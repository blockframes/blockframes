Our expertise

The Company

Cascade8 is the tech branch of the media company Logical Pictures Group. It develops a technological ecosystem to digitize, streamline and optimize the activities of financing, selling and acquiring audiovisual content.

The Applications

This Cascade8 ecosystem is composed of various applications, each of which aims at solving a specific bottleneck in the audiovisual value chain (Archipel Market , Archipel Content , Media Financiers )
These applications are linked to one another to simplify user access and data reusability between the different services.

Blockchain Projects

To enrich web and software projects, Cascade8 and the Logical Pictures Group are spearheads in the application of blockchain technology in the content industry, from rights & revenue management (Blockframes) and NFTs (FilmSeriesNFT.com) to STOs (through its tokenized investment fund Logical Content Ventures).
