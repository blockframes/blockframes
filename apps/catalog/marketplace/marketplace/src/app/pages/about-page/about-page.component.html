What is Archipel Content ?

Archipel Content is the first marketplace offering package deals for TV and Film rights. The Archipel Content platform gives Content Buyers access to a large consolidated library, composed of several Sales Agents’ titles. That way, we offer Content Buyers a way to purchase more titles in a more efficient way, and Sales Agents the chance to create new opportunities by mutualizing their assets. Our goal is to streamline the whole licensing process: by offering an efficient tool to trade rights, while instantaneously redistributing revenues to the rightful stakeholders, thanks to Blockframes, a blockchain-powered value chain.

What is Blockframes?

Blockframes is a technological ecosystem for the film industry, aiming to bring transparency and efficiency to independent cinema. Concretely, Blockframes replicates a film’s value chain on the blockchain, to streamline all transactions linked to the film’s lifecycle and waterfall.

Sounds nice... but what is the blockchain exactly?

“The blockchain is an incorruptible digital ledger of economic transactions that can be programmed to record not just financial transactions, but virtually everything of value.” – Blockchain Revolution (2016) by Don & Alex Tapscott. Basically, it’s a time-stamped, immutable, transparent, secured and shared database. What’s so special about it is that it brings decentralization, transparency, traceability, security, consensus, trust, automation, efficiency… Concepts that we could undeniably use in the cinema industry!

Where does this project come from?

The Blockframes project comes directly from the experience and expertise of our mother company, Logical Pictures. Logical Pictures is an innovating film production and financing company, launched in 2016 by serial entrepreneur Frédéric Fiore and his partner Yannick Bossenmeyer. As they were still new to the cinema industry, they identified quite a few bottlenecks at different levels of the value chain. With a non-formatted and open mindset, they started looking for solutions to answers these inefficiencies. They got particularly curious about the blockchain technology and quickly realized all the opportunities that it could offer to the cinema industry. They developed the idea of Blockframes and launched a successful proof of concept on the animation film Terra Willy in 2018. In 2019, they launched Cascade8, the start-up company that’s now in charge of developing these blockchain-powered tech solutions for the cinema industry. With Blockframes as a background, we’re developing a suite of DApps (“Decentralized Apps”: basically, blockchain-powered apps) that interact with each other. Archipel Content is one of them.
