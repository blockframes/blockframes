A "cookie" is a small text file containing data that is recorded on a terminal (computer, tablet, smartphone, etc.) when You browse a website.

The terms "You" / "Your" refers to the natural person reading this Cookie Policy, whose Personal Data is being processed.

The terms "We" / "Us" refers to CASCADE8.

There are two main categories of cookies:

  • Our cookies, i.e. the cookies We place on Your terminal. We generally use them to identify users, make it easier for them to navigate the site and save their choices.
  • Cookies placed by a third-party supplier. These cookies may be used by the third party to recognize Your terminal when You visit other websites.

1. Our cookies

We use cookies and other similar tracking devices such as tags and web beacons on the Websites to:

  • Obtain information about Your preferences, Your online movements and Your use of the Internet in order to produce statistics and improve the ergonomics of the Websites, detect design problems on the Websites, communicate with You and build Our customer relationship. To do this, We use proven third-party solutions that respect your Personal Data;
  • Conduct statistical research and analysis to improve the content of the Websites and Our Services and to help Us better understand Your expectations and needs as an User. To do this, We use third-party solutions to analyze the statistics of visits to the Websites and to track Your interactions with the pages of the Websites;
  • To make Your online experience more functional and enjoyable

Precisions

If You have accepted the storage of cookies on Your browser, the cookies embedded in the pages and content You have consulted may be stored temporarily in a dedicated area of Your browser. They will only be readable there by the sender. The length of time this information is kept varies from a few minutes to two years.

2. The cookies provided by third-parties

We work with third party suppliers such as Google Analytics who may also install cookies on the Websites for anonymized statistical and tracking purposes. These third parties are responsible for the cookies installed on the Websites.

Precisions

The information collected via these third-party cookies may be transferred outside the European Union because some cookie suppliers have servers all over the world in order to ensure a better quality of service. If You would like more information, please consult the confidentiality policies of these third parties, which are available on their websites.

3. Categories of cookies

Essential cookies or cookies that do not store Personal Data

You cannot refuse them as they enable the Websites to function

These cookies are essential to the operation of the Websites and cannot be deactivated in Our systems. As they are strictly necessary for the provision of the Websites, You cannot refuse them without this having an impact on the operation of our Websites. In general, these cookies are only set in response to actions that You perform, such as defining Your preferences in terms of confidentiality, language or filling in forms. You can configure Your browser so that it blocks these cookies or alerts You to them, but certain parts of the Websites will not function. These cookies do not store any personally identifiable information.

Functional cookies (experience and relationship)

You may refuse them, but this could affect your browsing experience on the Websites

These cookies are used to improve the functionality of the Websites. They may be set by Us or by third-party suppliers whose services We have added to our Websites. You can refuse these cookies by not giving Your consent. If You do not authorize these cookies, some or all of these services may not function correctly.

Analysis, performance and personalisation cookies

You may refuse them

These cookies enable us to count visits and sources of traffic so that we can measure and improve the performance of our Site. They help us understand how our Site is used so that we can make changes to our Site to improve your experience. These are non-essential cookies managed by the cookies banner. You can refuse these cookies by not giving your consent.

Marketing cookies de marketing, for targeted advertising

You may refuse them

These cookies are used to make advertising more relevant to You. They may be used by Our advertising partners to profile Your interests and show You relevant advertising on other sites. They do not directly store personal information but are based on the unique identification of Your browser and internet device. You can refuse these cookies by not giving Your consent. If You do not authorize these cookies, You will see less targeted advertising.

Cookies have an important role in the operation of our Services. Deactivating them could (particularly in the case of technical cookies) have the effect of reducing or preventing access to all or part of our Services.

If You are not satisfied with the use made of cookies:

  • You can delete them easily by accessing Your browser's cookies folder. You can also configure Your browser so that it blocks cookies or sends an alert message before a cookie is installed on Your computer.
  • You can find out more, configure Your cookies, and accept or reject them at any time by visiting the Webites' cookie management page, the link to which can be found at the bottom of each page of the Websites (footer). You will be able to indicate Your overall preferences for the Websites', either by purpose or by third-party service provider.
