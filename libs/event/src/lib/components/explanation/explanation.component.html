How It Works

To organize a Screening on our platform, you need to set up the privacy settings (choose who will be able to access your Screening) and how much time your Screening will be active. You are also able to invite guests to your Screening and consult the attendance statistics in real time.

To organize a Meeting on our platform, you need to set up the privacy settings (choose who will be able to access your Meeting) and the time of your event. Also, during the creation of your Meeting you can invite your collaborators and add the the files that you would like to share during your Meeting (line-ups, promo reels etc.)
