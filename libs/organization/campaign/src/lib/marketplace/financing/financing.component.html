What is the return on investment in cinema industry?

Your Return on investment is your share of the total amount of proceeds resulting from the distribution of the film on all media around the world. It may be calculated in % by dividing your share of revenue by the initial investment. If the result is superior to 100%, this means your investment was profitable.
